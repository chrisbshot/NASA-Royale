About Us

At NASA Royale, we elevate the standards of food production and processing. From sustainable farming to quality food packaging, we are committed to excellence.














Our Services


Pig Farming

At NASA Royale, we are dedicated to raising healthy and well-nurtured pigs. Our farm focuses on sustainable and humane practices, ensuring that our pigs are raised in a clean and comfortable environment. We take pride in the quality of care provided to our livestock, which directly contributes to the superior quality of pork products we offer. Our commitment to excellence ensures that we meet the highest standards in the agro-processing industry, making NASA Royale a trusted name in Ghana's pork production sector.



Food Processing


At NASA Royale, we produce high-quality shito and Tom Brown. Our shito, made with a unique blend of spices, comes in various sizes for personal and commercial use. Our nutritious Tom Brown porridge mix, made from roasted maize and peanuts, is also available in different sizes, catering to all needs. We are committed to delivering quality and authentic taste in every product.



Global Logistics


NASA Royale offers fast, reliable, and nationwide delivery for all your orders. Whether you're buying fresh poultry, processed food, or farm products, we ensure timely and safe delivery right to your doorstep. Our service features real-time tracking and affordable shipping rates. Order now and enjoy the convenience of hassle-free delivery!
